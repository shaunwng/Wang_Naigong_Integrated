INTRODUCING Chef Shaun

Chef Shaun re-invents fine cuisine by combining heart-warming comfort food with Scandinavian classics. His professional French training have influenced his techniques and inspired a fresh perspective on flavours, texture and aromas.

Chef Shaun and his team of diversely experienced cooks create dishes that simple, classic, clean and elegant on to plate. Working with local farmers and producers, ingredients are sourced to be in season and sustainable adding extra comfort to each dish.

Savour the best of Scandinavian nature food !

Chef Shaun.
